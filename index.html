Reading the tiles ›
Three number suits (1–9) plus honor tiles. With Beginner mode on, dots and bamboo show their real countable faces and a faint number in the corner; honor tiles show an English tag. A winning hand is five sets + one pair (a set is three of a kind, or three in a run within one suit; honors can't form runs).

How scoring connects to Module 1 ›
The Discard Trainer teaches pure speed to a finished hand — but the fastest hand is often a cheap one. This module teaches the other half: which shapes are worth chasing.
